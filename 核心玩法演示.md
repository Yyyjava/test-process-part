### 核心玩法介绍演示稿 / Core Gameplay Introduction Script (预计时长 / Estimated Time: 90s)

**第一阶段：开场与直接引入 / Phase 1: Opening & Direct Introduction (约 / Approx. 15s)**

**【动作提示 / Action Cue】**：
打开游戏，直接切入第一关界面，将键盘/鼠标递给老师。
Open the game, skip directly to the level 1 interface, and hand the keyboard/mouse to the teacher.

**【口述 / Script】**：
“老师好，我是负责核心玩法介绍的同学。为了让您获得最直观的游戏体验，我们直接跳过菜单，进入第一关的教学关卡。请您亲自上手试玩。”
"Hello Professor, I am the student responsible for introducing the core gameplay. To give you the most intuitive experience, we will skip the menu and jump straight into the first tutorial level. Please go ahead and try it out yourself."

**第二阶段：轻度旁白伴游 / Phase 2: Light Guided Walkthrough (约 / Approx. 30-45s)**

**【动作提示 / Action Cue】**：
站在侧边，观察老师的操作。不要过度指挥，只在老师走到对应提示处或稍微卡顿时进行轻微补充。
Stand to the side and observe the teacher's actions. Do not over-direct; only provide slight prompts when they reach specific cues or hesitate.

**【口述 / Script】**（根据进度适时穿插 / Interject as needed based on progress）：

* （基础移动时 / During basic movement）：
“本关主要展示‘录制与幻影’的协作机制。基础的移动和跳跃操作，界面上都有清晰的指引。”
"This level primarily demonstrates the cooperative mechanism between 'Recording and Phantom'. Clear instructions for basic movement and jumping are displayed on the screen."

* （遇到交互时 / Upon encountering an interaction）：
“走到这里，您可以按 E 键进行交互。”
"When you reach here, you can press the E key to interact."

* （遇到录制时 / Upon reaching the recording mechanic）：
“这里便是我们的核心机制。（请按 C 键尝试录制一段动作，再按 R 键进行播放。）”
"This is our core mechanic.（ Please press the C key to record your actions, and then press the R key to play it back.)"

* （接近过关时 / Nearing the end of the level）：
“非常好，最后利用幻影按下按钮、激活电流，大门开启后即可通关。”
"Excellent. Finally, use the phantom to press the button and activate the current. Once the door opens, you can proceed to the next level."

**第三阶段：核心机制总结 / Phase 3: Core Mechanic Summary (约 / Approx. 30s)**

**【动作提示 / Action Cue】**：
老师通关或放下控制后，你接回话语权，面对台下进行总结。
After the teacher clears the level or puts down the controls, take back the floor and face the audience for the summary.

**【口述 / Script】**：
“非常感谢老师的试玩。正如大家所见，刚才画面中的粉色小方块就是玩家录制出的‘幻影’。
"Thank you very much for trying it out, Professor. As everyone just saw, the pink cube on the screen is the 'Phantom' recorded by the player.

我们设计的核心理念，是让玩家自主去 **organize（组织和规划）** 幻影的操作，使其与本体默契配合。
The core concept of our design is to require the player to **organize** the phantom's actions themselves, making it cooperate seamlessly with the main character. 

如果玩家逻辑清晰、对机制理解深刻，把幻影 organize 得很完美，关卡就会变得非常简单——就像刚才的叠跳，轻松就能借力跃上高台。
If the player's logic is clear and their understanding of the game is deep, and they organize the phantom perfectly, the level becomes very simple—just like the jump stacking we saw, you can easily use it to leap onto high platforms.

反之，如果没有 organize 好，比如录制出的幻影无规律乱动，那么跳跃的容错率就会极低，变得极其考验即时操作。这种‘策略规划’与‘操作反馈’的深度结合，正是这套机制最大的乐趣所在。”
Conversely, if not organized well, or instance, if the recorded phantom moves around randomly—finding the right timing to jump becomes extremely difficult, heavily testing the player's real-time execution. This deep integration of 'strategic planning' and 'operational feedback' is exactly where the greatest joy of this mechanic lies."
